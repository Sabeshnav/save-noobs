Tips from seniors to crack AEEE :

  • Understand and study the basics of all the concepts mentioned in the AEEE information handbook.
  • Use NCERT textbooks for better understanding.
  • Though the AEEE is not as tough as the JEE, you need to workout many problems from various sources to secure good rank in AEEE.
  • Be cool at the Examination and try answering all the questions.
  • Don't be afraid about the AEEE, be confident and prepare well. Can't wait to see you in our campus!. All the very best!!

Eligibility for AEEE :

  • Age : Candidates, whose date of birth falls on or after July 1, 1999.
  • Educational Qualification : A pass in 10 + 2 (Class XII) or its equivalent examination with minimum 60% aggregate of marks in Mathematics, Physics and Chemistry and with not less than 55% individually in each of these three subjects.

Life at Amrita

Academics

Amrita is one of the best universities in India. As students we feel that it's worth every penny you pay. Professors here are very talented and they help you in every possible way to become a better person both academically and emotionally. Amrita utilizes all possible technology to enlighten it's pupil. Professors help you in your projects in all the possible ways. All you need is a focussed mind and love towards learning new things. There are a lot of clubs which help you in developing your coding skills, speaking skills and what not? You can access any lab to learn and experiment things with the concerned faculty's permission. Amrita has a huge library which contains books for everything you need and the library provides a peacefull environment to study. Seniors will also help in all possible ways. Everything combined Amrita exposes you to a world class education.

Hostel Life

When we are all set to go to college we get so excited. That excitement is mostly not "WOW!! I'm going to learn new things.." but "I'm going to enjoy the hostel life with my new friends!!". Yes, hostel life plays an important role in your life, you get exposed to people from various places, learn a lot of new things and most importantly you learn to live independent. Amrita university has seperate hostels for girls and boys. The hostels are always kept clean, you can't complain about hostel maintenence to your parents. Every hostel block for UG contains rooms which can accomodate 4 students. The rooms are well ventilated with 2 windows. There will be no difficulties in using the lavatories. Every hostel is managed and taken care by friendly and helping wardens, you can feel free to talk about your difficulties to them. Every hostel has small cricket pitches and basket ball courts around it which will keep you away from getting bored. Hostels here have study rooms too. Amrita university serves its students with healthy and varieties of vegetarian food in clean and hygienic mess halls. The whole campus is clean and green which adds glitters to your hostel life. Totally Amrita hostel is one of the best hostels in the country.

Other amenities

Amrita coimbatore campus is really a huge campus and it has an enormously big playground. The university facilitates students to learn many games. The physical education department provides all the required equipments. You can find many small playgrounds near your hostels to play with your friends. The university provides gym for boys and girls seperately. The environment here will make your morning joggings very pleasant. General stores stores here have all the requirements a student need. Medical facilities are very good in case of any emergency serious protocols will be followed. Canteens here sell good snacks and food in a decent rate. There are many reprography centers and a garments shop which sells basic clothings. Amrita university encourages students to participate in various club activities. Clubs here help you to meet people from various parts of the country and help you to show your talents to others.

FAQ

What is it like to be a first-year student in Amrita university?

We have always enjoyed the days when we were in the very first year. The seniors were so sweet to us and they helped us out in all hard situations, so you won't find any kind of difficulties as a first year student. And wandering all around the University is recommended by us to know more and get familiar with the university.

Will I be ragged?

The university holds strict rules against ragging, and also the students here are very sweet and disciplined. You won't be ragged and you can't rag anyone else.

Is Amrita university safe for girls?

Absolutely!! Amrita is one of the safest place for girls. Unlike other universities Amrita teaches us human values. Every single person here is good hearted and respect others.

How good are the transportation facilities?

The coimbatore campus is situated very close to the ettimadai railway station. There are a good number of trains from campus to palakkad/coimbatore junction. You can always find auto rickshaws otside the campus which will take you anywhere you want.
