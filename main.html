Admission to B.Tech programmes offered at Amrita Schools of Engineering – Amritapuri, Bengaluru, Coimbatore, Chennai, Amaravati* is on the basis of one of the following:

  • Rank scored in Amrita Entrance Examination - Engineering 2020
  • Rank scored in JEE Mains 2020.
  • Acceptance of SAT Scores for B.tech Admissions.

Tips from seniors to crack AEEE :

  • Understand and study the basics of all the concepts mentioned in the AEEE information handbook.
  • Use NCERT textbooks for better understanding.
  • Though the AEEE is not as tough as the JEE, you need to workout many problems from various sources to secure good rank in AEEE.
  • Be cool at the Examination and try answering all the questions.
  • Don't be afraid about the AEEE, be confident and prepare well. Can't wait to see you in our campus!. All the very best!!

Eligibility for AEEE :

  • Age : Candidates, whose date of birth falls on or after July 1, 1999.
  • Educational Qualification : A pass in 10 + 2 (Class XII) or its equivalent examination with minimum 60% aggregate of marks in Mathematics, Physics and Chemistry and with not less than 55% individually in each of these three subjects.

For more information about Admission process visit Amrita university's official Admissions webpage.
